**Executive AI Suite: The Efficiency Engine**

"Because time is the only resource you can't buy more of."



🎯 The Core Mission

The Executive AI Suite is a data-driven diagnostic tool designed to identify "Time Leaks" in any schedule. Whether you are managing a corporate calendar or a busy personal life, this app quantifies inefficiency and provides a clear roadmap to recovery.



💼 For the CEO (Executive Mode)

In a corporate environment, "Meeting Fatigue" is a silent profit killer. This suite applies logic to your calendar data to:



* Flag High-Risk Meetings: Automatically identifies sessions with 10+ attendees and no recorded KPI.
* Quantify Recovered Time: Calculates exactly how many hours can be returned to the "Deep Work" budget.
* Visual ROI: Provides a high-contrast efficiency chart for immediate stakeholder reporting.



🏠 For the Daily User (Personal Mode)

For "normal" life, the app shifts from meeting analysis to Focus Filtering. It helps you visualize your day as a "Time Budget":



* Identify Micro-Distractions: Flags activities that exceed 30 minutes without a "Learning" or "Wellness" goal.
* Batch Processing: Detects fragmented errands and suggests grouping them to save "Travel Time."
* The "30% Rule": Visually proves if your day is being "stolen" by low-value habits, helping you recover 2+ hours for what matters.



**🚀 Technical Stack**

* Language: Python 3.12 (via Anaconda)
* Data Library: Pandas (High-speed data scrubbing)
* Visualization: Matplotlib (Executive-grade charting)
* Environment: Jupyter Notebooks
